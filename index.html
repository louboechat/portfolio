About me

Luiza Boechat

BizOps Process Design & Automations Internal Workflow Optimization

I build the systems that keep companies from falling apart operationally.

I work in operations — specifically the part where things are still a mess. Processes that live in someone's head, tools that aren't connected, teams that spend more time managing information than doing actual work.

I come in, figure out what's actually broken, and build something that fixes it properly. That usually means a system in Coda, an automation in Zapier, or a custom tool in Base44 — whatever makes the most sense for how the team actually works.

I've built recruitment pipelines, CRMs, team management hubs, time tracking systems, and a lot of automations. The common thread is that they all get used, because they're built around real workflows, not ideal ones.

Past managers have described me as someone who brings order to work that would otherwise stay messy. That's probably the most accurate thing anyone has said about what I do.
